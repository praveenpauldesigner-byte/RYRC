The Word

What does it mean to Remember?

To remember is to keep something in your mind so you can recall it when needed — not just a passing thought, but a deep knowing you live by daily.

The same principle applies to God. The more you intentionally keep Him at the center of your life, the more natural it becomes to walk with Him.

🔁

Repeat It

Frequent use strengthens memory. Daily time with God builds a lifetime habit.

❤️

Feel It

Emotionally meaningful things stick. Let your faith become something you love, not just know.

⭐

Value It

We remember what we consider important. Make God your highest priority.

Your Season

Why Youth Is Your Superpower

Your brain is literally still being built. The prefrontal cortex — the part that handles decisions, impulse control, and moral reasoning — doesn't fully mature until age 25.

WHO and UN define youth as ages 15–24 — the most moldable, formative window of your entire life. What you build now, lasts.
